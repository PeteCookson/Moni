How to use Moni crypto valuation tool

Moni Cryptocurrency Valuation Tool gives a real time view of your crypto assets.

  • Select and enter the relevant cryptocurrency holdings you own.
  • Once you have entered all your cryptocurrency holdings to 8 decimal places, press calculate.
  • The information returned shows the spread of investment and real time current value.
